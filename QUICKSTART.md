## How to Use Your Habit Tracker

### 1. Open the App
Simply open `index.html` in your web browser by double-clicking it.

### 2. Try These Steps

**First Habit:**
1. Type "Drink 8 glasses of water" in the input box
2. Click "Add Habit"
3. See it appear in the list with 0 day streak
